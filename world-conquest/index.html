🌍 Welcome to World Conquest!

Your goal: conquer the entire world region by region. You start in Western Europe with a small army and limited resources.

👆 Click any region on the map to select it and see its details on the right panel.

⚙️ Economy First

Each region you own produces resources every second. Open the Economy tab on the left to research Agriculture — it will boost your food production and unlock more options.

💡 Resources are shown in the top bar. Watch your Gold (💰) — it's used for almost everything.

🪖 Build an Army

Go to the Military tab and recruit some Militia. They cost 15💰 + 3🌾 each. Train at least 5–10 before attacking.

⚔️ Your total military power is shown at the bottom of the Military tab. Higher power = easier conquest.

🗺️ Attack a Neighbor

Select North Africa or Iberia on the map (they border you). Click Attack in the right panel, choose your force, and fight!

🏆 Reach Phase 2 by owning 12% of the map (5 regions). New techs and units will unlock!

⚔️ How Combat Works
• Rounds auto-fire every 5 seconds — both sides deal damage each round.
• Click STRIKE! anytime to build up bonus damage (applied at round end).
• Your HP = combined power of your deployed troops.
• Units are permanently lost when you take damage.
• Retreat anytime — but you'll lose units proportional to damage taken.
