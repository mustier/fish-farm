Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ClickingGame.com ("the Site", "we", "us") handles information when you play any of our free browser games at clickingame.com.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Each game stores progress data locally in your browser only (via localStorage), such as:

  • Game progress (resources, buildings, upgrades)
  • Game settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Advertising

Our games use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show personalized ads based on your browsing activity.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policies

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

3. Cookies

Our games do not use cookies directly. However, Google AdSense may place cookies on your device for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements.

4. Third-Party Services

Our games may load the following third-party resources:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Fredoka One, Nunito)
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • html2canvas (Cloudflare CDN) — for screenshot/share features

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
